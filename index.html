Who We Are

The HomeMakers

Connecting homes. Empowering talent. Serving communities.
A hyper-local digital marketplace enabling home-based entrepreneurs to monetize their skills — from home kitchens to handmade products and neighbourhood services.

Our Story

From Household Talent to Professional Growth.

Homemakers didn't start in a boardroom — it started in the kitchen, the studio, and the heart of the home. We saw incredible talent everywhere: cooking, crafting, organizing — hidden in plain sight. These were professional-grade skills with real market value, held back only by the awkwardness of modern neighbourhood selling.

The Problem

Posting in society WhatsApp groups felt like spamming friends.
Community apps like MyGate or NoBroker felt intrusive and disorganized.
Managing orders through chat meant lost details, endless follow-ups, and zero professional clarity.

A Dignified Digital Bridge

The skill was always there — and so was the demand. What was missing was structure. The Homemakers replaces the noise with a dedicated platform, transforming scattered local selling into a trusted digital marketplace.

Showcase with Confidence — Build a professional profile that does the talking for you.
Manage Seamlessly — Handle orders and payments without the back-and-forth of informal chats.
Connect Directly — Reach verified buyers in your own community through a dignified, purpose-built channel.

No more awkward broadcasting. No more informal clutter. Just a clear path for your talent to grow.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision we make — from product to people to purpose.

01

Dignity in Every Interaction

We believe selling from home should feel confident, not uncomfortable. Every homemaker deserves visibility without awkward promotion or informal chasing.

02

Structure Creates Confidence

Unorganized selling creates hesitation. A clear platform, transparent pricing, and defined processes build trust for both sellers and buyers.

03

Community First

We grow neighbourhood by neighbourhood. Local talent serving local demand creates stronger, self-sustaining communities.

04

Empowerment, Not Charity

Homemakers is not about helping someone survive. It’s about giving capable individuals the wings to grow as micro-entrepreneurs.

05

Trust is Non-Negotiable

Verified profiles. Clear communication. Transparent transactions. Trust is the foundation of every order.

06

Small Beginnings, Scalable Vision

We start from one home, one society, one community — but we build systems that can scale across cities.

07

Respect for Home Talent

Every listing represents time, effort, and personal skill. We honor the value of household productivity.

08

Simplicity Wins

Technology should remove friction, not create it. The platform must feel easy, intuitive, and supportive.

09

Continuous Improvement

We listen to homemakers and customers alike. Feedback drives evolution.

10

Growth with Integrity

We expand responsibly, ensuring fairness, transparency, and long-term sustainability.
