Who We Are

About The HomeMakers

Connecting homes. Empowering talent. Serving communities. Homemakers is a hyper local digital marketplace that enables home-based entrepreneurs to monetize their skills — from home kitchens and handmade products to essential neighbourhood services. We believe every home holds talent. We simply bring it to the community.

Our Story

From Household Talent to Professional Growth.

Homemakers didn't start in a boardroom; it started in the kitchen, the studio, and the heart of the home.

We saw incredible talent—cooking, crafting, and organizing—hidden in plain sight. These were professional-grade skills with real market value, yet they were being held back by the awkwardness of modern "neighborhood" selling.

The Problem with the Status Quo

Before we built this bridge, local entrepreneurs faced a frustrating dilemma:

The Hesitation: Repeatedly posting in society WhatsApp groups felt like "spamming" friends. The Friction: Promoting on community apps like MyGate or NoBroker felt intrusive and disorganized.

The Chaos: Managing orders through unstructured chats meant lost details, endless follow-ups, and a lack of professional clarity.

A Dignified Digital Bridge

The skill was there, and the community demand was even stronger. What was missing was structure.

The Homemakers replaces the noise with a dedicated platform. We’ve transformed scattered local selling into a trusted digital marketplace where you can:

Showcase with Confidence
Build a professional profile that does the talking for you.

Manage Seamlessly
Handle orders and payments without the back-and-forth of informal chats.

Connect Directly
Reach verified buyers in your own community through a dignified, purpose-built channel.

No more awkward broadcasting. No more informal clutter. Just a clear path for your talent to grow.

👩‍🍳

A Platform, Not a Push

Homemakers is not about “helping” someone earn. It is about giving them visibility, structure, and confidence. It allows any homemaker to become a micro-entrepreneur — with their own profile, listings, pricing, and identity — without stepping outside their home. Because sometimes, all talent needs is a runway.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

The principles that guide every decision we make — from product to people to purpose.

01

Dignity in Every Interaction

We believe selling from home should feel confident, not uncomfortable. Every homemaker deserves visibility without awkward promotion or informal chasing.

02

Structure Creates Confidence

Unorganized selling creates hesitation. A clear platform, transparent pricing, and defined processes build trust for both sellers and buyers.

03

Community First

We grow neighbourhood by neighbourhood. Local talent serving local demand creates stronger, self-sustaining communities.

04

Homemakers is not about helping someone survive. It’s about giving capable individuals the wings to grow as micro-entrepreneurs.
